ARTIST STATEMENT

Growing up, there was always media that I enjoyed, but there were never specific images or pieces that I felt truly connected to. I found myself searching through Pinterest for ideas—whether for future tattoos, artwork, or simply something I wanted to hang on the wall in my room.

That inspired me to start creating. I wanted to become that outlet for other people: someone who could take ideas, emotions, and experiences and turn them into something unique and personal. Sometimes people know exactly how they feel but don't know how to express it visually, and that is where I come in.

I create for people, and through that process, I help them express themselves more fully and authentically. I love creation in all mediums, whether through fine art, illustration, tattoo concepts, or commissioned work.

You are your own brand. Let me help you individualize yourself.

— JoLesa Ann Birkhead
Fine & Studio Artist
