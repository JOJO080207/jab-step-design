ARTIST STATEMENT

My work explores storytelling through fine art, studio practice, illustration, and tattoo-inspired design. I am drawn to images that carry emotion, memory, and personal meaning, whether I am creating a traditional artwork, a tattoo concept, or a commissioned piece.

As a fine and studio artist, I use visual storytelling to connect ideas, symbols, and lived experiences. Tattoo culture influences the way I think about permanence, identity, and personal expression, while illustration allows me to shape those ideas into clear and meaningful visual forms.
